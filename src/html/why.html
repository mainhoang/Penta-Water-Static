family-owned

Penta is family-owned and operated exclusively in sunny Southern California. It’s this patented, 13-step purification process that makes our water so unique. 13 step patented process

It's the science behind Penta that makes our water ultra-pure. In fact, Penta's filtration is so unique it's patented by the United States Patent and Trademark Office. Every bottle of Penta water undergoes a 13-step/11-hour purification process to remove more impurities than any other bottled water.

  • micron filtration

    pharmaceutical grade to remove volatile organic compounds and other contaminants

  • reverse osmosis

    high pressure filtration to remove salt, minerals and additional impurities

  • deionization

    ion exchange to filter out remaining minerals

  • ultraviolet light

    to remove 99.99% of microorganisms

  • ozonation

    infused with ozone, a tasteless molecule made from atmospheric oxygen, to naturally sterilize

The end result is an ultra-purified water that is arsenic-free, chlorine-free, chromium 6-free, fluoride-free, MTBE-free, pharmaceutical-free and BPA/BPS- free with typical dissolved solids of less than one part per million. Please see our water quality report for additional information.

naturally balanced pH

The optimal pH range for our bodies is between 7.35 and 7.45. Only when our body's pH level is within this range can we effectively assimilate the vitamins, minerals, and food supplements we need to survive. More and more health conscious consumers are becoming aware of the harmful effects of an acidic diet and are seeking solutions to bring their body fluids within an acceptable range.
