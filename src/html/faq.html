what is penta water?

Penta is ultra-purified bottled water. A patented, 13-step purification process makes our water so unique. It takes over 11 hours to meticulously filter every drop from start to finish. The end result is our proprietary, ultra-pure water free from harmful additives, pollution and impurities.

What sets Penta water apart from other bottled water?

Our water is simply that, pure, 100% natural water. Penta is arsenic-free, chlorine-free, chromium 6-free, fluoride-free, MTBE-free, pharmaceutical-free and BPA/BPS-free with typical dissolved solids of less than one part per million. Penta is naturally alkaline without chemicals or artificial additives. It is water its very best.

Does Penta add any chemicals or artificial additives during the purification process?

No. After we source our water form its natural underground aquifer, our patented 13-step/11-hour filtration process begins to remove impurities, etc. We do not add chemicals to alter the pH of our water nor do we add other additives such as chlorine, which is a widely used chemical to disinfect drinking water. Chlorine can generate carcinogenic byproducts, such as trihalomethanes and haloacetic acids, which may pose health risks.

(US EPA Source: http://wwter.epa.gov/drink/contaminants/basicinformation/disinfectants.cfm)

Is arsenic, chlorine, chromium 6, fluoride, MTBE, pharmaceuticals or pesticides found in Penta water?

No. Penta Water is free from arsenic, chlorine, chromium-6, fluoride, MTBE, pharmaceuticals, pesticides and many more trace impurities such as lead. Our patented filtration process effectively detects and filters out trace contaminants that some public water systems and bottled water companies simply cannot or do not because of cost and lack of technology.

Does Penta water have minerals?

No. Penta water is ultra-purified with typical dissolved solids of less than one part per million. Our goal is instant hydration and high mineral content water can slow down this process and cause bloating or fullness.

What is Penta's pH level?

Penta water has a naturally alkaline pH. The pH level of our water is achieved as rain and snow percolate down high mountain peaks. The water collects underground beneath a protective seal of solid rock and clay and no alkaline altering chemicals or additives are added.
